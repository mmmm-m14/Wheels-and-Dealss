BMW Z4 M40i Description Page

Overview

The BMW Z4 M40i is a high-performance variant of the Z4 roadster, known for its powerful engine, sporty handling, and luxurious features. It combines the thrill of open-top driving with the performance of a sports car.

Performance and Specifications

  • Engine: 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine.
  • Power: Produces 382 horsepower.
  • Transmission: 8-speed automatic transmission with paddle shifters.
  • Acceleration: 0-60 mph in approximately 3.5 seconds.
  • Top Speed: Electronically limited to 155 mph.

Interior Features

  • Cabin Quality: Luxurious interior with high-quality materials and finishes.
  • Seating: Sport seats with Merino leather upholstery.
  • Infotainment: 10.3-inch touchscreen with navigation, Apple CarPlay, and Android Auto.
  • Sound System: Harman Kardon surround sound system for an immersive audio experience.

Safety and Technology

  • Safety Features: Includes forward collision warning, automatic emergency braking, and lane departure warning.
  • Driver Assistance: Optional features like adaptive cruise control and blind-spot monitoring.

Pricing

The starting price for the BMW Z4 M40i is approximately $71,125, depending on options and packages.
