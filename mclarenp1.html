Overview

The McLaren P1 is a groundbreaking hybrid hypercar that pushed the boundaries of automotive engineering. Combining ferocious power with cutting-edge aerodynamics and advanced hybrid technology, the P1 delivered an unparalleled driving experience.

Performance

Under the hood, the Mclaren P1 is powered by a 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine generates 737 horsepower, complemented by an electric motor delivering an additional 179 horsepower. * Turbocharging: The twin-turbochargers provide rapid boost response, minimizing turbo lag. * IPAS (Instant Power Assist System): This electric motor system delivers an immediate surge of power, enhancing acceleration and eliminating turbo lag.

Interior Features

The P1's interior isn't just about being fast; it's about making the driver feel connected to the car and the road. It's a place where you feel like you can truly push the limits.
