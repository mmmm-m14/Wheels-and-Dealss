Ferrari Roma

Experience the Elegance

The Ferrari Roma is a modern grand tourer that combines elegance and performance.

Overview

The Ferrari Roma is a stunning representation of Italian craftsmanship and engineering. With its sleek lines and powerful stance, it embodies the spirit of the Ferrari brand. Designed for those who appreciate the finer things in life, the Roma offers a perfect blend of style, comfort, and performance.

Performance

Under the hood, the Ferrari Roma is powered by a 3.9L V8 twin-turbo engine that delivers an exhilarating driving experience. With a power output of 611 hp and a torque of 561 lb-ft, the Roma can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.4 seconds. The car features a sophisticated suspension system and advanced aerodynamics, ensuring exceptional handling and stability at high speeds.

Interior Features

The interior of the Ferrari Roma is a masterpiece of design and technology. It features luxurious leather upholstery, customizable ambient lighting, and a state-of-the-art infotainment system. The driver-focused cockpit includes a digital instrument cluster and a large touchscreen display for easy access to navigation, music, and vehicle settings. Comfort is paramount, with spacious seating and high-quality materials throughout the cabin.

Specifications
